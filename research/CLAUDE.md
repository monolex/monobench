# Monobench Research Claude Guide

This is a durable research archive for monobench experiment logs and comparative analysis.

Follow the same rules as `AGENTS.md` in this directory.

## Operating Mode

- Evidence first.
- No speculation without a status label.
- Preserve raw logs.
- Write concise, comparable analysis.

## Preferred Output Shape

For each case analysis:

1. Current result table.
2. Ground truth summary.
3. Per-run answer comparison.
4. Successful path.
5. Failure paths.
6. Tool-behavior diagnosis.
7. Monogram strengthening candidates.
8. Regression fixture proposal, if useful.

## Do Not

- Do not edit `raw/`.
- Do not overwrite old analysis; add a dated file.
- Do not collapse remembered results into archived results. Record discrepancies explicitly.
